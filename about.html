Our Story

37 Years of Application Expertise

Founded in 1987, Gerotech started as a CNC machinery distributor with a simple belief: manufacturers deserve a partner who understands their floor, not just a vendor who takes their order.

Over the decades, we've expanded from distribution into engineering — building an in-house team that modifies, customizes, and automates around the machines our customers already own. Today, Gerotech operates from three Michigan locations, serving manufacturers across the state and beyond.
